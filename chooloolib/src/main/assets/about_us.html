Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?

To sign up for AMA, download and install the AMA app from the Google play store. Follow the registration process and enjoy our services.

How do I sign out?

To sign out or stop using our services, go to the apps setting menu and click sign out.

How do I get paid?

If your account wallet has earned you more than our minimum withdrawal amount of Ksh 150, you can go to the settings menu on the app and select withdraw. The amount will be sent to you within 48 hrs

How long does it take to get money after withdrawal?

Intakes 48hrs at most to process and make payments to your account

What is the minimum withdrawal amount?

Ksh 150

How do I listen to the ads?

The ads will be played when you are (call waiting) and you will get paid Ksh 1.5 per advertisement listened to.

How else can I listen to ads apart from call waiting?

You can listen to ads as ringtones if you allow ringtone ads in the settings.

Why am I not getting ads to listen to?

If you are not getting ads to listen to, it means there are no ads suited to your demography running at that particular time.

How do I contact you?

Go to the app settings and select the Contact Us button. From there you can get in touch with Us through your preferred means

How do I unsubscribe from ringtone ads?

Go to the settings menu and deactivate ringtone add.

How do I opt out of listening to some ads?

Go to the settings menu, select preferred ads, and uncheck/deselect the ads category you don't like.
